## Overview

Jungle is a 3D environment built in Unity, featuring realistic terrain, vegetation, and animal life. It was developed to explore Unity's asset and scripting systems.

## Key Features

- **3D Terrain**: Realistic landscape with trees, grass, and water.
- **Animals & Assets**: Includes animals, paths, and interactive elements.
- **Unity Systems**: Uses TextMeshPro and Input System.
